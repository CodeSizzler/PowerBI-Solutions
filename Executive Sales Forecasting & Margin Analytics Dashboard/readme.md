# Executive Sales Forecasting & Margin Analytics Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview
This repository showcases a high-impact **Sales Forecasting and Margin Analytics** solution developed at **CodeSizzler**. Designed for executive-level decision-making, the dashboard transitions beyond traditional sales tracking to provide deep insights into **Margin Erosion** and **Predictive Revenue Trends**.

Built using **Power BI** and aligned with **Microsoft Fabric** architectures, this project demonstrates our expertise in building data-driven "Executive & Employee Experience (EXE) Suite" tools that prioritize both performance and security.

---

## 🏗 Business Use Case: "Margin-First" Sales Strategy
**Industry:** Enterprise Retail / Financial Services (Mortgage Domain focus)

### **The Challenge**
Organizations often face the "Growth Paradox"—sales volumes increase, but net profitability declines due to unmonitored operational costs, aggressive discounting, and fluctuating market conditions. Traditional reports often lag, showing margin loss only after the quarter ends.

### **The CodeSizzler Solution**
We developed an **Early Warning System** within the sales dashboard that:
* **Predicts Future Revenue:** Utilizes time-series forecasting to project sales for the next 90 days.
* **Identifies Margin Erosion:** Real-time calculation of "Cost to Serve" vs. "Gross Revenue" to highlight transactions that are "Margin Dilutive".
* **Executive Entry Canvas:** A streamlined view for leadership to identify risk indicators against established company norms instantly.

* **Security:** Implements **Secure AI by Design** principles, ensuring sensitivity labels and row-level security (RLS) are governed via **Microsoft Purview**.

## 📊 Key Features
1.  **Predictive Sales Engine:** Dynamic forecasting with adjustable confidence intervals.
2.  **Margin Protection KPI:** A dedicated metric that flags regions where customer acquisition costs threaten profitability.
3.  **Drill-Through Intelligence:** Seamlessly move from a global sales overview down to individual loan or product-level performance.
4.  **AI-Powered Insights:** Integrated "Smart Narratives" that summarize key drivers of sales variance automatically.
